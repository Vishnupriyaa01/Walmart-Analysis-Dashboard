                                    WALMART ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

Introduction

This project analyzes Walmart’s sales performance using a dataset from Kaggle. The goal was to understand how sales vary across store types, cities, departments, and time. The analysis also examines seasonal patterns such as holiday vs non-holiday performance. A fully interactive dashboard was created using Excel, which helps track weekly sales, compare store categories, find top-performing departments, and study sales trends over the years.

Project Objective

To study Walmart’s sales trends from 2010 to 2013.
To compare performance across store types A, B, and C.
To identify top-performing departments based on revenue.
To understand whether holidays influence sales.
To build an insightful dashboard that supports management decisions.

Key Insights

1️) Store Type Performance

Store Type A contributes the highest share of sales (approximately 64%).
Store Type B follows with 30%, while Store Type C contributes around 6%.
This indicates Walmart relies heavily on its large-format Type A stores.

2️) Sales Trend Over the Years

Sales increased steadily from 2010 to 2011.
Slight decline in 2012.
Sharp drop in 2013, possibly due to fewer records or changes in operations.
Overall, Walmart maintained strong early-year momentum.

3️) Holiday vs Non-Holiday Sales

Holiday Sales (52%) slightly outperform Non-Holiday Sales (48%).
This shows customers spend more during major festivals and promotional periods.

4️) Top Performing Departments

The top 10 departments show sales between 0.2B – 0.5B.
Department 92 and 95 stand out with sales close to 0.5B.
Indicates these departments drive significant revenue.

5️) Weekly Sales Behavior

Weekly sales vary widely between stores.
Multiple stores show sales above 0.3B, highlighting high-performing locations.
Smaller stores show lower but consistent weekly sales.

Conclusion

This Walmart Sales Analysis provides a comprehensive view of how the company performs across different store categories, cities, departments, and time periods.
The dashboard helps identify:
High-performing store types and departments,Yearly and seasonal sales patterns,Revenue-driving periods like holidays,Variation in store-level weekly sales
Overall, the insights help Walmart’s management in better planning inventory, allocating resources, optimizing store formats, and strategizing seasonal promotions.
